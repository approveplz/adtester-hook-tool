How to Use This Tool

  • Upload your main video.
  • Select one or more hook videos from the list below.
  • Enter the name of your ad.
  • Click the upload button to start the process. Your main video will be stitched with each of the selected hook videos. This will take a minute or two.
  • Your ads will be named using your provided ad name, appended with -HOOK-randomId and be uploaded to Facebook in a few minutes.
  • Note: this currently only works with vertical 9:16 main videos and will upload to the Vincent CC X 1 Roofing Ad Account

How It Works

This tool automatically combines the selected videos and uploads the final videos as Facebook ads using the existing Facebook ad testing app and automation.
It uses Creatomate to merge the videos, then triggers Make.com webhook events that download the processed videos and places them in the designated Google Drive folder, where another Make.com scenario will upload them as a Facebook ads.
